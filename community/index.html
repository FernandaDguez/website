Join the Popper community

Popper is an open source project that anyone in the community can use, improve, and enjoy. We'd love you to join us! Here's a few ways to find out what's happening and get involved.

Learn and Connect

Using or want to use Popper? Find out more here:

  • Google Groups: Discussion and help from your fellow users
  • Twitter: Follow us on Twitter to get the latest news!
  • Stack Overflow: Practical questions and curated answers

Develop and Contribute

If you want to get more involved by contributing to Popper, join us here:

  • GitHub: Development takes place here!
  • Slack: Chat with other project developers

You can find out how to contribute to Popper in our Contribution Guidelines.
